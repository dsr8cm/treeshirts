Meet the TreeShirts Activist of the Month of November 2019!


Dr. Eric Stoloff

For years now, Dr. Stoloff has been working tirelessly to protect the Amazon, which is why we chose him as our activist of the month! Ever since earning his doctorate in environmental science from the University of Virginia, Eric has been on the front lines in Washington, D.C, lobbying for congress to put an end to the rampant deforestation in the Amazon by American corporations. When he isn't busy with saving the world, Dr. Stoloff enjoys walking through the forests behind his house, playing with his german shepherd Bailey, and visiting the botanical gardens. Make sure to thank him, because without heroes like Dr. Stoloff, our mission to save the forests would be a lot harder!

Our Most Recent Efforts



For the past few months, TreeShirts has been working closely with the indigenous Yanomami tribe, planting trees in the rainforest along the border between Venezuala and Brazil. Thanks to our brave volunteers and customers like you that fund us, we've been able to plant over 1,000 trees since we arrived! We've also spent a great deal of time learning about the rich culture of the Yanomami, whose unique way of life is dependent on the health of the rainforest. With your help, we can preserve their home, and let their people thrive.
