Our Idea

We decided to make a difference in the world by founding Tree-Shirts, an online clothing store that truly cares about trees. Our pledge to our customers is that for every shirt you purchase on our website, we will plant a precious new tree in the Amazon rainforest. We'll take care of the planting, all you need to do is buy one of our fashionable yet comfortable shirts. The future of the planet is in our hands, and it's up to us to make it a better place for us all to live.

All of our shirts are made from natural, sustainable materials, and we refuse to manufacture in countries where child labor is legal. By being an online-only business, we don't waste precious energy and resources on unnecessary brick and mortar locations. When you buy a shirt from Tree-Shirts, you know what you're getting is 100% green and sustainable, and that's our personal guarantee.

Since starting out, Tree-Shirts has already planted over 15,000 trees in the Amazon, and we're showing no signs of slowing down. By buying one of our shirts, you can join the ever-growing Tree-Shirts family, and together we can make a difference in the world.
